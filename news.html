News
18.11.2019: Neurotech blogpost on our workshop

The last workshop day - and with out workshop on Cognitive Vehicles - concluded this year's IROS conference. We would like to thank all invited speakers, contributing authors as well as all participants for their contribution making our workshop a success. You can find a nice summary on our workshop at the webpage of Neurotech, one of our sponsors, at https://neurotechai.eu/post/25-towards-cognitive-vehicle-workshop-bio-inspiration-helpful.

15.11.2019: Presentation slides on the webpage

We are currently collecting and iteratively uploading the presentation slides of our invited speakers' talks. You can find them under https://cogvehicles2019.github.io/speakers.html by clicking on the speaker's abstract.

07.11.2019: Proceedings available

We are happy to announce, that the proceedings containing the papers presented during the poster sessions of our workshop are now available online. You can download the proceedings from https://cogvehicles2019.github.io/assets/proceedings/proceedings.pdf

06.11.2019: Important! Updated schedule

IROS 2019 is well under way. As we are approaching our workshop on cognitive vehicles, we now set up our final program. You can find the final program at https://cogvehicles2019.github.io/program.html. The workshop will be held at room LG-R11 (according to IROS’ room numbering) or, equivalently, Capri 1104 (according to the Venetian’s room numbering system).
